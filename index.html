Welcome, Bienvenidx ! I am a multidisciplinary artist, educator, and community worker with a focus on intergenerational learning, and play-based education. I work with earth to create ceramics and sculptural masks, incorporating creative movement and land-based teachings into person-centered wholistic wellness practices for children and youth. I am also the co-founder of Cecilu, a jewellery shop based in Toronto, exploring our connection to our creative process and mental well being.
